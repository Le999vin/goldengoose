About Us

Golden Goose was founded in Venice, Italy, in 2000 by Alessandro Gallo and Francesca Rinaldo. The brand is renowned for its handcrafted shoes that blend vintage design with modern aesthetics. Each pair of shoes undergoes individual treatment to achieve a unique, lived-in look through specialized finishing techniques. Despite its international success, Golden Goose remains true to its Italian craftsmanship, with production continuing in selected workshops in Italy. The brand has evolved into a comprehensive lifestyle label, offering not only footwear but also apparel and accessories, focusing on quality, innovation, and stylish design. Golden Goose embodies the unique spirit of Venice and has firmly established itself as a symbol of artisanal perfection and timeless style.

Story about the Super-Stars

It all began with Golden Goose's first sneaker, adorned with a star on the side, famously known as the "Super-Star," which swiftly ascended to become one of the brand's most legendary models. By 2007, Golden Goose had already been in existence for seven years, yet its journey into the realm of sneaker design was only just beginning. The introduction of the Super-Star marked a pivotal moment in the sneaker market, injecting new vitality into the world of athletic footwear. Its distressed, lived-in appearance stirred considerable attention, setting it apart from other models and heralding a new era of innovation that departed from the tidy, polished styles of the time.
